Cello Lessons

Susan teaches both children and adults at the Cello Connection Studio in Eau Claire.

Susan is available to teach during regular school hours as well as weekday afternoon and evenings. Home school children can make cello lessons part of their home school day while school children and working adults can schedule convenient lesson times during the afternoon or evening. At this time, both virtual lessons and in-person lessons are being offerred. Day and evening summer lesson times are also available.

30 Minute Lessons (suggested for preschool through grade 6)
45 Minute Lessons (suggested for grade 7 through adult)
