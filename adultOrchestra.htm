The String Connection - Eau Claire, Wisconsin



The Full Connection Concert, Eau Claire, WI, May 9, 2015
The String Connection, is an adult intermediate level string orchestra, with string players ranging from the beginning to advanced playing levels. The orchestra rehearses at "The Cello Connection" cello studio in Banbury Place, Thursday nights for an hour and a half, approximately fourteen times per session. The String Connection has been playing together since fall, 2007.

With a "NO Audition" policy, new musicians are now being accepted for the spring session. Prerequisites include: must be age 18 or older, must be able to play 1st position notes in simple keys (knowledge of simple extensions helpful and 1st-3rd positions helpful, but not required) and must enjoy playing!

"The Full Connection", an adult intermedite level full orchestra, will consist of woodwind/brass musicians to join the already formed "The String Connection" musicians.
